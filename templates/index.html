About

From Roman coins to fossils and priceless gems, here's a guide to hidden treasure in the British countryside.

Over the decades a significant number of ‘British treasure troves’ have been discovered across the United Kingdom. Most of them were located either by accident or by the patient work of enthusiasts with metal detectors. In most cases these treasures were hidden by their owners during times of social and political upheaval. Although many such discoveries have already been made it is widely believed that many more exist waiting to be revealed.
